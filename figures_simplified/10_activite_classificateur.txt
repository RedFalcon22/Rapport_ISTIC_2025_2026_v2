activity diagram - the classifier receives a text from the Netethic platform and preprocesses it by removing URLs, normalizing spaces, and converting to lowercase. It first tries the LLM-only strategy with a direct zero-shot call. If the LLM responds correctly the result is used. If not, it tries the LLM with legal grounding by retrieving relevant legal passages from ChromaDB and injecting them into the prompt. If that also fails it falls back to keyword-based heuristics. If heuristics also find nothing the text is labeled as unclassified. After getting the labels from whichever strategy worked, the classifier checks if any label indicates suicidal ideation and sets an urgency flag if so. It then logs the result and returns the labels, confidence, strategy used, urgency flag, and latency to the platform.
