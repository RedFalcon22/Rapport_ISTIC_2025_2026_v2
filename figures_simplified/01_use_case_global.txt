use case diagram - the Netethic AI system has 4 human actors: a Visitor (public site), an Internal User (platform), an Administrator, and the Netethic System. It also has 5 AI actors labeled <<Actor>>: Noa, NetEthic Support Assistant, Emotional Support Module, Harassment Classifier, and LLM Server. The Visitor can ask a question on the public site and book a demo, both handled by Noa. The Internal User can ask a question internally, schedule a support appointment, report an incident, and receive emotional support. The Netethic System submits texts to the Harassment Classifier. The Administrator supervises the system stats and caches. All AI actors that need language generation call the LLM Server. The Administrator supervises Noa and the Support Assistant.
